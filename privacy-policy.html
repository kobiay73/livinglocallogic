We use your contact information solely to respond to your enquiry, provide details about our programs, and follow up regarding your application. We do not use your data for unrelated marketing purposes.

We retain your information for as long as needed to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at logic@livinglocallogic.com.

Cookies
