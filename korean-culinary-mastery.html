The Three-Day Program

What you will be able to do
when you leave.

No lectures. No PowerPoints. Every technique is performed by your hands, on real ingredients, corrected in real time by Chef Lee Tak.

01
Day One · July 22

Breakdown & Craft

You will break down whole salmon and chicken from scratch — understanding muscle structure, aging potential, and zero-waste utilization. By the end of this day you can execute Korean-style butchery, skewering, and charcoal grilling with the confidence to teach it to others.

02
Day Two · July 23

Fermentation & Aging

You will master salt-curing (Shiojime), kelp-pressing (Kombujime), and Ganjang-based preservation with your own hands. You leave knowing how to apply Korean aging techniques — Yeoneojang, Saeujang — in your own kitchen or menu, with the depth to explain the logic behind every step.

03
Day Three · July 24

The Korean Table

You will build a full Korean table — seasonal namul, traditional kimchi, banchan — from fermentation principles up. You leave with a framework for Korean culinary logic that you can apply to any menu, any kitchen, anywhere in the world.
