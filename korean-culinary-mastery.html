The Three-Day Program

What you will learn — and practice — every day.

No lectures. No PowerPoints. Every technique is performed by your hands, on real ingredients, corrected in real time by Chef Lee Tak.

01
Day One

Breakdown & Craft

Master the breakdown of whole salmon and chicken — muscle structure, aging potential, zero-waste utilization from the first cut to the final dish. Korean-style skewering, charcoal grilling, and the discipline of nothing wasted.

02
Day Two

Fermentation & Aging

Salt-curing (Shiojime), kelp-pressing (Kombujime), and Scandinavian Gravlax reinterpreted through a Korean lens. Ganjang-based preservation. The craft of Yeoneojang and Saeujang. Time as an ingredient — not a shortcut.

03
Day Three

The Korean Table

Seasonal namul, traditional kimchi, and banchan through a plant-based lens. The foundation of Korean culinary logic — fermentation as daily practice. Taught the way Chef Lee Tak's mother taught him.
